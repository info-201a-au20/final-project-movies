1991


In 1991, the World Wide Web was introduced along with the internet. This served as a significant change in how information could be accessed, shared, and created. Considering the doors that were opened in this process, 1991 was one of the years that was analyzed.


In 1991, the highest rated genre was Biography and the lowest rated genre was Crime. These do not point to a certain reason in terms of world wide events.


1991 served as a turning point in terms of the budget and average USA income explored by movies. While before and during 1991, these were between 10 and 20 million. After 1991, these values, budget and income, jumped by 10 million and 70 million respectively. Since the introduction of the internet opened new opportunities for the world, the movie industry also saw a growth following the year. In this analysis, 1992 was excluded since only one movie with incomplete numbers were reported in our dataset.
